QRParty Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

QR Party ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the QR Party mobile application and website at partywithqr.com.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, and zip code. Your zip code is used to show you local events in your area.

Event Data

When you create an event, we collect the event title, date, time, location, description, event type, and any wishlist or checklist items you add.

RSVP Data

When you RSVP to an event, we store your RSVP status, the number of guests you are bringing, and any checklist items you claim.

Photos

Photos you upload to the Picture Wall are stored securely and are only visible to guests of that specific event.

Usage Data

We collect basic usage information to improve the app, including device type and app version.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your events and RSVPs
  • Show you local events near your zip code
  • Send host notifications to event guests
  • Improve the QR Party experience

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your name and RSVP status are visible to the host of events you attend and to other guests of those events.

Event details marked as public are visible to other QR Party users in your area on the Explore tab.

We use Firebase (Google) to store your data securely. Google's privacy policy applies to data stored in Firebase.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your profile information in the Settings screen
  • Delete your account by contacting us at privacy@partywithqr.com
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

QR Party is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.
