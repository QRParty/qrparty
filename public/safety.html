Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 6, 2026 · QR Party LLC

QR Party LLC is committed to maintaining a safe and trusted platform for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content of any kind.

Our Commitment

QR Party does not allow, facilitate, or tolerate any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors. This includes images, videos, text, or any other material that constitutes child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns directly to us at any time by emailing admin@partywithqr.com. All reports are reviewed promptly by QR Party LLC.

Reports may also be submitted through the app using the in-app contact and support options available in Settings.

Compliance and Reporting to Authorities

QR Party LLC complies with all applicable child safety laws. Any identified CSAM or CSAE content will be reported immediately to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) via its CyberTipline at missingkids.org, as well as to relevant regional and national authorities.
