The Builder

One person. One laptop. Four real products.

I'm Chris — a solo app developer based in Seaside, CA, building under Klaus Custom Studio. No investors, no VC, no team. Just a dad with a laptop, a methodology that works, and a habit of shipping things that actually solve problems in the communities I live in.

"I don't build apps for the App Store. I build tools for the brewery down the street, the PTA across town, the riders at the trailhead."

Every app I build starts with a real problem I've seen up close. QR Party came from watching event hosts manage RSVPs in group texts. Wrench Market came from the chaos of finding a trustworthy mechanic. RollCall came from the mess of coordinating OHV rides on Facebook.

I build fast using Flutter, Firebase, and AI-assisted development — and I've turned that process into its own product called Scaffold, so other developers can build the same way.
