How It Works

THREE STEPS.
THAT'S IT.

QR Party strips away the complexity so you can focus on having fun.

🎉
Create Your Event
Name it, date it, describe it. Add a wishlist so guests know exactly what to bring. Done in under a minute.
📲
Share Your QR Code
Every event gets its own unique QR code. Print it, text it, or post it — your guests scan to RSVP instantly.
📸
Collect the Memories
After the party, everyone's photos land in one shared wall. No more hunting through a dozen group chats.
