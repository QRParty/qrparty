Delete Your Account

QR Party LLC · admin@partywithqr.com

You can delete your QR Party account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below to submit your deletion request.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

1
Open the QR Party app and sign in to your account
2
Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner of the home screen
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account
4
Confirm the deletion when prompted — your account will be permanently removed
⚠️ This action is permanent Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, events, RSVPs, photos, and all associated data. This cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

• Your profile (name, email, account settings)
• Events you've created and all associated RSVPs
• Photos you've uploaded
• Your wishlist and checklist contributions
• Your organization or business account and linked data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days in backups before being permanently purged. Anonymized aggregate data (such as event attendance counts) may be retained indefinitely but cannot be linked back to your identity.

Can't Access the App?

If you're unable to access your account to delete it in-app, email us at admin@partywithqr.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We'll process your request within 7 business days.
